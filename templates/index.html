The Science of Fraud Detection

How our Random Forest / Decision Tree model evaluates your data.

Pattern Recognition

The model analyzes the correlation between the amount sent and the depletion of the origin account. Fraudulent agents often empty an account entirely in a single "TRANSFER" followed by a "CASH_OUT".

Anomaly Detection

By comparing the oldbalance and newbalance, the AI detects if the transaction actually occurred as stated. Discrepancies in these balances are a primary indicator of synthetic fraud.

Understanding the Features

Feature Description
Step Unit of time (1 step = 1 hour). Useful for detecting high-frequency attacks.
Type Method of transfer. "TRANSFER" and "CASH_OUT" are statistically higher risk.
Balances Used to verify the mathematical integrity of the sender and receiver accounts.
